# About Europe Weekly

Europe Weekly is your weekly dose of European news and insights. Every weekday, we cover four European countries with concise news summaries and fascinating fun facts.

## What We Cover

Each episode features:
- **Daily News Scripts**: Current events and politics from four European countries
- **Fun Facts**: Interesting cultural, historical, and geographical insights
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: From Portugal to Poland, Norway to Greece

## Schedule

- **Monday**: Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Ireland
- **Tuesday**: Poland, Latvia, Lithuania, Estonia  
- **Wednesday**: Germany, France, Belgium, Netherlands
- **Thursday**: Portugal, Spain, Italy, Greece
- **Friday**: Bulgaria, Slovenia, Romania, Croatia
- **Saturday**: Czechia, Slovakia, Hungary, Austria
